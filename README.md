## What is EcoHeal?

EcoHeal is an online platform that connects people across India and the world with nature. We bring together nature lovers, researchers, and communities to seek sustainable, nature-based solutions for everyone.

We are an early-stage, independent, self-funded organisation — built with genuine love for nature and a belief that small communities can create real change.

---

## What We Do

### 📖 Silent Reading Sessions
We organise silent reading programmes and eco awareness camps — quiet, focused sessions that build a habit of learning and reflection around nature. Our first session is coming soon at **Mansarovar, Jaipur**.

### 🔬 Research & Sustainable Solutions
We conduct independent research and provide sustainable solutions for MSMEs, industries, NGOs, and government bodies seeking nature-based answers.

### 🏕️ Eco Adventure Tours
Nature treks, river and waterfall visits, wildlife experiences — organised by the EcoHeal community.

### 📚 Nature Content Hub
Articles and research written in-house covering:
- Environment & Ecology
- Basic Concepts (Species, Organisms)
- Climate Change
- Pollution
- Biomes ,
-sustainable develoment
